Frequently Asked Questions

How do I restore my progress?

Your game progress is stored locally on your device. Unfortunately, if you delete the app or switch devices, your progress cannot be recovered. We recommend keeping the app installed to preserve your streaks and statistics.

How does the daily challenge work?

A new puzzle is available every day at midnight in your local time zone. Complete it to build your streak! If you miss a day, you can watch a short video ad to unlock that day's puzzle and continue your streak.

How is my score calculated?

Your score is based on: puzzle difficulty (Hard gives more points), completion time (faster = more points), and your current streak (bonus points for consecutive days). Daily challenges also give bonus points!

Can I play offline?

Yes! Sudoku Owl works completely offline. You only need an internet connection if you want to watch an ad to unlock a missed daily challenge.

How do I turn on Night Mode?

Tap the Settings icon (gear) at the bottom of the home screen, then toggle "Night Mode" on. This enables a darker theme that's easier on your eyes for evening puzzling.

What do the hints do?

Hints reveal the correct number for a selected cell. Tap on any empty cell, then tap the "Hint" button to see the answer. Use them wisely - they're there to help when you're truly stuck!

How do I use Notes mode?

Tap the "Notes" button to enable pencil marks mode. When active, tapping numbers will add small candidate numbers to the selected cell instead of filling it in. Tap Notes again to return to normal mode.

What does the star calendar show?

The Daily Calendar shows your completed daily challenges with gold stars. Green circles mean completed, orange circles mean missed. Your monthly progress percentage is shown at the top.
