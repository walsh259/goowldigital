Frequently Asked Questions

What is Tabata training?

Tabata is a high-intensity interval training (HIIT) protocol consisting of 20 seconds of intense exercise followed by 10 seconds of rest, repeated for 8 rounds (4 minutes total). It was developed by Japanese scientist Dr. Izumi Tabata and has been shown to improve both aerobic and anaerobic fitness.

How do I start a workout?

From the Home screen, tap "Start" to begin a classic Tabata workout. Or go to the Workouts tab to choose from preset workouts or create your own custom intervals.

Can I create custom workouts?

Yes! Premium users can create unlimited custom workouts with their own work time, rest time, number of rounds, and sets. Tap the "+" button on the Workouts tab to create a new custom workout.

How do I restore my purchases?

Go to Settings and tap "Restore Purchases". This will restore any previously purchased subscriptions or lifetime access to your current device.

Will the timer work in the background?

Yes! Owlet continues to run even when you switch to another app or lock your screen. You'll hear audio cues and feel haptic feedback for phase transitions so you never miss a beat.

How are calories calculated?

Calorie estimates are based on the duration and intensity of your workout using average HIIT calorie burn rates. These are approximations and actual calories burned may vary based on your weight, fitness level, and exercise intensity.

How do I cancel my subscription?

Subscriptions are managed through Apple. Go to Settings → Apple ID → Subscriptions on your device to manage or cancel your Owlet subscription. You'll retain premium access until the end of your current billing period.

What's included in Premium?

Premium includes: unlimited custom workouts, full workout history (free tier shows last 7 workouts), ad-free experience, and priority support. Choose monthly or lifetime access based on your preference.

My workout history disappeared!

Workout data is stored locally on your device. If you've reinstalled the app or switched devices, previous data cannot be recovered. We recommend keeping the app installed to preserve your workout history and streaks.

How do I turn off sounds or vibrations?

Go to the Settings tab and toggle "Sound Effects" or "Vibration" off according to your preference. You can also enable or disable voice countdown for the final seconds of each interval.
